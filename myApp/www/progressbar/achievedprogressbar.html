Eco Milestone Tip

The most important factor in eco-driving is then the way in which you drive. Driving more smoothly means driving more fuel efficiently, so you should avoid accelerating or braking aggressively. Your driving will be more eco-friendly if you accelerate smoothly, getting in to a high gear as quickly as possible. Never over-rev your vehicle in low gears. Similarly, it is better to slow down at a junction or lights by taking your foot off the accelerator and allowing the engine breaking to reduce your speed, than it is to pelt up to the lights and brake hard when you get there.
